# Code of Conduct

Offline Games Hub welcomes contributors of every background and experience level.
We want this to remain a friendly place to make small games, learn, and help one
another.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Discuss the work, not the worth of the person doing it.
- Welcome beginners and explain feedback clearly.
- Respect privacy, authorship, licenses, and personal boundaries.
- Keep the default library family-friendly.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, threats, hate speech, sexualised attention, deliberate intimidation,
doxxing, spam, and attacks based on identity or ability are not accepted. Do not
publish another person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments and contributions, close discussions,
or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when these rules are
broken. Enforcement decisions should be fair, private where possible, and
focused on keeping the community safe.

Report conduct problems privately to the repository owner through their GitHub
profile or GitHub's reporting tools. Do not put sensitive personal details in a
public issue. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.
